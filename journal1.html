Introduction

Hello! I hope you, the reader of my personal journal, are doing well. My name is Jonathan Wang and I am currently a freshman in college. I attend Cornell University and I am majoring in Computer Science in the College of Arts and Sciences. I am not entirely sure what I am interested in yet within this field of computer science, but I am curious and open to explore!

My hobbies include playing video games, exploring new cuisines, working out, fashion, dancing, and listening to music. One of my dreams is to travel the world and take photographs of amazing sights.
